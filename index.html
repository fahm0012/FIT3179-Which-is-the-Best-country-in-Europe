Brief Introduction About Europe:

Europe is a continent located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere and mostly in the Eastern Hemisphere.Europe covers about 10,180,000 square kilometres (3,930,000 sq mi), or 2% of the Earth's surface (6.8% of land area), making it the second smallest continent. Europe has a total population of about 741 millionpeople (about 11% of the world population) as of 2018.
